Your idea, **INFUX**, has the potential to be much more than a college project if it's executed well. Here's a detailed breakdown.

# What is INFUX?

INFUX is a platform that connects **brands** and **influencers/content creators** for marketing collaborations.

Today, many brands struggle to find the right creators, and many creators struggle to find sponsorships. INFUX solves this by acting as a trusted marketplace.

Think of it as:

* LinkedIn for professional creator networking
* Fiverr for influencer services
* Upfluence for influencer discovery
* CreatorIQ for campaign management

---

# The Problem

## For Brands

Brands often ask:

* Which influencer is genuine?
* Which influencer fits my audience?
* How much should I pay?
* How do I track campaign results?
* How do I avoid fake followers?

Most businesses, especially small businesses in Nepal, don't have easy access to influencer marketing tools.

---

## For Influencers

Influencers often face:

* No consistent sponsorship opportunities
* Difficulty reaching brands
* Unclear payment processes
* Lack of a professional portfolio

Many creators rely on Instagram DMs and personal contacts.

---

# The Solution

INFUX creates a centralized ecosystem.

### Brand Workflow

1. Brand creates account
2. Brand creates campaign
3. Brand specifies:

   * Budget
   * Audience
   * Platform
   * Location
   * Goals
4. AI recommends suitable creators
5. Brand sends offers
6. Creator accepts
7. Campaign runs
8. Payment is released
9. Both parties leave reviews

---

### Influencer Workflow

1. Creator registers
2. Connects social accounts
3. Builds portfolio
4. Receives campaign suggestions
5. Applies or accepts offers
6. Delivers content
7. Gets paid
8. Builds reputation score

---

# Why This Can Work in Nepal

The creator economy is growing rapidly.

Many Nepali brands use:

* Facebook
* Instagram
* TikTok
* YouTube

But influencer partnerships are often managed manually through messages and calls.

INFUX could become a localized creator marketplace for Nepal before expanding internationally.

# Core Modules

## 1. Authentication System

Users can:

* Register
* Login
* Reset password
* Enable 2FA
* Verify email

Roles:

* Admin
* Brand
* Influencer

---

## 2. Influencer Profiles

Each creator profile contains:

* Profile photo
* Bio
* Social media links
* Niche
* Audience demographics
* Engagement rate
* Portfolio
* Reviews
* Creator score

Example:

```text

Niche: Technology
Followers: 150,000
Engagement: 7.8%
Location: Nepal
```
